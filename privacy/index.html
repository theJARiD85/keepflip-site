Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

KeepFlip is an AI-assisted resale platform that helps people evaluate items, prepare them for sale, and generate resale-related insights. This Privacy Policy explains what information KeepFlip collects, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

KeepFlip is currently designed for resale planning, item organization, and listing preparation. This policy should be updated before adding payments, buyer-to-seller transactions, advertising technology, or a public marketplace.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information when you use KeepFlip:

  • Account information: your name, email address, account identifier, and password authentication information handled through our account provider.
  • Item and listing information: item titles, brands, models, category, condition, notes, photos, estimated values, listing drafts, and related details you choose to enter.
  • Analysis information: descriptions of an item’s condition or issue, symptoms, repair-related information, and results returned by KeepFlip’s analysis tools.
  • Optional location information: approximate location information only when you grant permission for a nearby repair-provider search.
  • Technical and usage information: device, app, diagnostic, and log information needed to operate, secure, and improve the service.
  • Support communications: information you send when you contact KeepFlip Support.

2. How We Use Information

KeepFlip may use information to:

  • create and secure your account;
  • save and organize your resale assets;
  • generate item analysis, repair research, listing suggestions, and pricing-related guidance;
  • provide requested features, support, and service communications;
  • detect misuse, fraud, or security issues; and
  • improve KeepFlip’s features, reliability, and user experience.

3. AI and Service Providers

KeepFlip uses service providers to support the app. For example, we may use Appwrite for account, database, storage, and Function infrastructure, and AI service providers to generate analysis or listing content.

When you request an AI-powered feature, information relevant to that request—such as item details, photos, condition notes, repair symptoms, or listing inputs—may be sent to the provider operating that feature. Do not submit information you do not want processed for those purposes.

4. How We Share Information

KeepFlip may share information only as needed with:

  • service providers that operate features on our behalf;
  • professional advisers, auditors, or insurers when necessary;
  • law enforcement, regulators, or other parties when required by law or needed to protect rights, safety, or security; and
  • parties involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, or transfer of business assets.

KeepFlip does not sell personal information for money. KeepFlip does not use personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

5. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to operate KeepFlip, maintain your account, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. You may request deletion of your account or certain personal information by contacting us.

6. Security

KeepFlip uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures intended to protect information. No system can guarantee absolute security, so you should use a strong password and protect access to your device and account.

7. Your Privacy Choices and Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information, or to ask for information about our data practices. To make a request, email support@keep-flip.com from the email address connected to your KeepFlip account.

California residents may also have rights under California privacy law. KeepFlip will not discriminate against you for exercising applicable privacy rights.

8. Children’s Privacy

KeepFlip is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Contact us if you believe a child has provided personal information through KeepFlip.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as KeepFlip evolves. When we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date and may provide additional notice where required.

10. Contact Us

For privacy questions or requests, contact:
support@keep-flip.com
