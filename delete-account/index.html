Account management

Delete your KeepFlip account

You may request deletion of your KeepFlip account and associated personal data at any time.

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Request account deletion

To request deletion, email KeepFlip Support using the button below. Include the email address associated with your KeepFlip account.

Request account deletion Contact support

To protect your account, KeepFlip may request verification before completing a deletion request.

Data that will be deleted

Once your request is verified and processed, KeepFlip will delete your account and personal data associated with it from active KeepFlip systems, including:

  • Your KeepFlip account profile and account identifiers.
  • Your saved inventory records, item details, and notes.
  • Item photos uploaded to your KeepFlip account.
  • Your marketplace listings and listing-related information.
  • Repair, analysis, and resale-planning information associated with your account.

Data that may be retained

KeepFlip may retain limited information when necessary for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, dispute resolution, or to enforce our agreements.

Marketplace message records may be retained only when necessary to preserve another user’s conversation history, resolve a dispute, or meet a legal obligation. Any retained information will be limited to what is reasonably necessary for that purpose.

Deletion timeline

Verified account deletion requests are generally processed within 30 days. Information stored in secure backup systems may remain until those backups are routinely overwritten, for no longer than 90 days, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Questions or privacy requests

For questions about your account, privacy, or deletion request, contact us at support@keep-flip.com.
